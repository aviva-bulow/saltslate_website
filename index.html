Our Mission

We design nutritious food that fits within a low income budget. We help people plan, shop, and cook so that they save time, save money, and eat better.

Save Time


  • Get a new menu each month that's tailord to your household's government benifits, allergies, food restrictions, and tastes.

  • Use recipes that are designed as a group to maximize your efficiency.

  • Get customized shopping lists and detailed cooking instructions.


    • According to the USDA, SNAP and WIC participants spend more time cooking than any other demographic in the US.
    By helping people shop and cook more efficiently, we reduce their stress and improve their lives.


Save Money


  • Get delicious recipes that actually fit within your budget.

  • Maximize your benifits, and don't run out before the month is over.

  • Take advantage of seasonal abundance and buy things when they're on sale!
